Questions

Good to know

Is iQurious only for K-12 students?+

No. iQurious also supports ALS learners, certification and licensure exam takers, and university students — anyone who wants structured, verified academic support.

How does iQurious keep tutors accountable?+

Every session requires dual time-in and time-out confirmation from both devices, plus a post-session rating and reflection. Tutors who fall below the minimum quality threshold are reviewed.

What happens if my child's tutor is a stranger?+

All tutors are ID-verified and credential-checked before onboarding. For minors, guardians manage all bookings, payments, and messages — tutors only interact academically.

Can two students split one tutor's session?+

Yes — group sessions let connected learners book the same tutor together. Payment splits automatically and each learner's record stays accurate.
